关于我们 / About Our Restaurant

Step into Charcoal Grill Odyssey, your destination for an authentic grilling experience. Our menu highlights the Super Wolfe Overlord Meal, a robust selection of perfectly charred meats, and the Muwu and Baa’s Happy Meal, a favorite that offers a taste of our best skewered creations. Explore a variety of grilled meats and seafood, like our renowned House Special Lamb Skewer and the delightfully tender Charcoal Squid Skewer. Vegetarians can rejoice with our Enokitake and Potato Slice Skewers, ensuring there's delight in every bite for all.

Complement your meal with a sweet Pear Juice with Rock Sugar or a refreshing Green Tea Drink Honey Jasmine Flavor. Nestled at 298 Pine Avenue, we invite you to relish in the flavors that dance off our grill from 5 pm to 11 pm on Monday and Sunday. Join us for a journey through exquisite tastes and smoky aromas at Charcoal Grill Odyssey.
